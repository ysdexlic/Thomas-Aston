Thomas Aston graduated from the Tonmeister course at the University of Surrey in 2015 with First-Class Honours. He was awarded both the 'AMS-Neve Portfolio Prize' for the most outstanding final-year portfolio of music recordings and the 'Focusrite Prize for Technical Achievement' for the most outstanding dissertation.

Prior to graduating, Thomas enjoyed a year at BAFTA award-winning post-production house Aquarium Studios in Soho, where he had the opportunity to work on television, film and video-game audio for clients including the BBC and EA Sports.

Based in London, Thomas currently works on a large variety of projects ranging from music recording and production to video editing and audio post-production for film.
